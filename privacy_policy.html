We use collected information to:

3. Third Party Services

This app uses the following third party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database
  • Google AdMob — advertisements

These services have their own privacy policies. Please review them at policies.google.com/privacy

4. Advertisements

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device information to show relevant ads. You can remove all advertisements permanently with a one-time in-app purchase.

5. In-App Purchases

endlessTetris offers an optional one-time purchase to remove all advertisements permanently.
  • This purchase is processed through Google Play and is subject to Google Play's terms of service
  • Once purchased, ads will be removed from the app permanently
  • The purchase can be restored on any device using the same Google account
  • Purchases are non-refundable except as required by applicable law

6. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

8. Data Retention

Game scores and user data are retained as long as you use the app. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email below. Anonymous usage data is automatically deleted after 90 days.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.
